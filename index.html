About

Research first.
Craft always.

I'm a Product Designer based in Copenhagen with six years of experience designing complex, data-heavy digital products where clarity, trust and precision are critical. I've owned UX and UI end to end across multiple product areas — from opportunity framing and discovery through validation, delivery and iteration.

My strength lies in translating complex workflows and operational processes into intuitive, coherent experiences. I map the system logic first, then follow that thinking through to interface detail. Strong product thinking, a service design sensibility, and deep expertise in Figma and scalable design systems.

Outside of work I'm into martial arts, running, and hobbyist game development. Currently learning Japanese.
